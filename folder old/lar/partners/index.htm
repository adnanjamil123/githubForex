ABOUT PARTNERS

Spanning forty countries, our worldwide partner base contributes greatly towards the ethos of the company and is invaluable for extended client support services.

From our global offices in the United Kingdom, United Arab Emirates and Central and South East Asia, we view each partnership as different and strive to adapt to our partner's requirement at a local level towards an optimised business solution that best suits the target market in their region.

INTRODUCING PARTNERS

Our introducing program enables our partners to be awarded for referring their client network whilst simultaneously increasing their business profile.

Through our professionally account managed partnership services we offer access to our leading online trading facilities, a complete front-to-back office support infrastructure as well as business strategy and marketing consultancy.

  • Daily remuneration payments
  • Front-to-back office support infrastructure
  • Business strategy and marketing consultancy

ASSET MANAGERS

Our Managed Account Program software enables our experienced asset managers to trade for their client network quickly and efficiently from the comfort of one MT4 account.

All client sign-up processes are digitised within the client portal thus expediting the on-boarding process and assisting with ongoing account monitoring functionality.

  • Swift account program joining procedure
  • Automated performance and management fee calculations
  • Customisable and transparent comission structure

WHITE LABEL PARTNERSHIPS

One Financial Markets view white labelling as a key stepping stone for our regulated corporate partners towards a total integration of the market leading MT4 platform.

We offer a comprehensive product suite and a delivery service that can be seamlessly incorporated into your existing operations, thereby increasing both business and revenue potential under your exclusive company brand.

  • Fast integration of our award-winning brokerage services
  • Fully branded MT4 platform & client portal
  • Dedicated training and support

Counterparty Relationships

Our counterparty services are tailored to meet the demands of financial institutions, money managers, corporations and proprietary trading houses.

Whether the trading relationship is via one of our trading platforms or alternatively using our bridge services, we can provide institutional clients with a professional, reliable and secure solution.

  • Trade revenue and analysis
  • Platform bridge & connection solutions
  • Highly competitive pricing
